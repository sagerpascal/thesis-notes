\chapter{The Agentic Gap: A Survey of Agents for Computer Use}
\label{ch:acu-survey}

\section*{Preamble}
This chapter establishes the motivation for the thesis by evaluating the current state of autonomous agents. It identifies the ``Agentic Gap''---the disparity between current AI capabilities and the requirements for robust, autonomous digital interaction. This work establishes the baseline limitations that the subsequent chapters aim to address.

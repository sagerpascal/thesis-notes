\chapter{Pillar B: Learning Implicit Structure via Generative World Models}
\label{ch:world-models}

\section*{Preamble}
Complementing the architectural approach, this chapter explores a top-down, objective-driven paradigm. We demonstrate how deep generative World Models can learn implicit environmental rules and planning structures through long-term predictive objectives, leveraging scalability while addressing the planning deficits of current systems.

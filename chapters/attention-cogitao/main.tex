\chapter{Diagnosing Unstructured Reasoning: Attention Mechanics and COGITAO}
\label{ch:cogitao}

\section*{Preamble}
Following the identification of the Agentic Gap, this chapter investigates why current Transformer-based models struggle with robust reasoning. We establish a mathematical framework to analyze the underlying structures of self-attention and introduce the COGITAO visual reasoning framework to empirically demonstrate the limitations of unstructured learning in compositional domains.
